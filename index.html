About the Practice

A multidisciplinary practice built on taste, restraint, and confidence.

Studio Melez is an award-winning, internationally recognized design practice. Named to the AD100, the studio's work has been featured in Architectural Digest, Elle Decor, and The World of Interiors.

The practice approaches each project holistically — architecture, interiors, and decorative arts considered as a single, unified gesture. The result is spaces that feel inevitable, as though they could not have been otherwise.
