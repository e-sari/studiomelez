The Studio

Studio Melez is a multidisciplinary design studio creating spaces that enhance human experience — through texture, material, and cultural memory. We believe great interiors are not decorated, they are built: from the structure of light to the weight of a surface, from the grain of a material to the silence between objects. Each project begins with careful listening — to the client, to the site, to the story that wants to be told. The result is spaces that feel both rooted and alive, shaped by craft, curiosity, and a deep respect for the made world.

About the Practice

A multidisciplinary practice built on taste, culture, and craftsmanship.

Our work is shaped by layered cultural references, creating spaces that feel both rooted and contemporary. The studio is recognized for its emphasis on craftsmanship and personal gestures, expressed through refined detail and intentional design.

The practice approaches each project holistically — architecture, interiors, and decorative arts considered as a single, unified gesture. The result is spaces that feel inevitable, as though they could not have been otherwise.

Ezgi Sari
Principal

Trained as an interior architect and furniture designer, Ezgi brings a rigorous spatial sensibility to her work — shaped by material exploration, cultural narratives, and a careful balance of texture, light, and proportion.
